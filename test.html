Coronavirus (COVID-19)

Corona virus is a fast spreading infectious disease caused by newly discovered coronavirus called SARS-CoV-2. The World Health Organization has declared this disease as a world wide pandemic. At this point, scientists are still studying the various aspects of this disease and evaluating potential treatment plans to cure this disease. Vaccinees are still on the developing stages. People affected with COVID-19 have been showing various symptoms but most common are respitory symptoms such as cough, fever etc. Age is one of the concerning factors for this disease. People with existing health conditions such as disbetes, heart conditions and other underlying medical conditions are likely to have a greater impact on health if tested positive of COVID-19.

Symptoms

Since it a newly discovered disease, people tested positive of this virus have been showing various symptoms. Most common sypmtoms are as follows.
  • Cough
  • Fever
  • Sore Throat
  • Tiredness
  • Shortness of breath
  • Loss of taste or smell
  • Skin rashes
  • Body pain
COVID-19 spreads from person to person through respitory droplets from an infected person via sneeze or cough. Scientists also believe that COVID=19 can be spread from touching surface or objects in which virus has been in contact with and also by touching their own mouth, nose and eyes. CDC ha advised people with mild symptoms to home quarentine themselves and seek medical attention for those presenting with severe symptoms. It is said that COVID-19 symptoms are shown only 5 to 6 days after being infected and can last upto 14 days.
