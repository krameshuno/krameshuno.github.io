Preventive Measures

As we are aware, the COVID-19 vaccinees are still on the testing stages and it will be months for the vaccinees to be avaialble for use. In the meantime we can take some preventive measures to help fight against COVID-19 and remain healthy. The CDC recommends few precautionary measures to help avoid exposure to this virus.
  • Wash your hands with soap and water for atleast 20 seconds or use hand sanitizer that has 60% alcohol content.
  • Cover your nose and mouth using a face mask in public.
  • Practise 6 feet social distancing everywhere in public.
  • Clean and sterilize habitually contacted items and surfaces every day.
  • Avoid close contact with sick people.
  • Use tissue when sneezing or coughing and throw the tiisue into the trash after use.
  • Stay home if sick.
  • Avoid touching your eyes, nose, or mouth with unwashed hands.
  • Practise good hygiene.
All over the world the concept of social distancing has been followed strictly in various fields. The concept of social distancing has been initiated to avoid the further spreading of COVID-19 or any other illiness in the communities. The practise of social distancing has been followed in the various fields in the following way.
  • Stores such as Walmart, Target, Costco etc have advised against shopping during peak hours. Infact these stores have alloted shopping hours for elderly people and others to avoid any risk of contracting infections. These stores have also limited the number of persons entering the stores and limited shopping hours as well.
  • Coorporate businesses have incorporated work from home concept. Company policies have also changed and is made flexible for the employees to work and communicate remotely.
  • People are advise against travelling long distances to avoid being exposed to the virus. If travel is compulsory, face masks are made mandatory for travel.
  • Events such as season games, concerts and shows are canceled to possible outbreak fo the the virus on large crowds.
